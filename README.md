## Projects

### 1. Customer Churn Analysis


![Python](https://img.shields.io/badge/Python-3.10+-blue)


![Pandas](https://img.shields.io/badge/Tool-Pandas-150458)


![Type](https://img.shields.io/badge/Type-EDA-green)


Analyzed customer churn behavior for a retail company across ~37,000 customer
records. Explored key factors driving customer departure including membership
type, complaint history, and customer feedback through structured EDA and
visualization.

**Key findings:** 54% churn rate | Membership type strongest predictor | Poor Product Quality #1 feedback  
**Tools:** Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn  
**Repo:** [customer-churn-analysis](https://github.com/profpius/customer-churn-analysis)
